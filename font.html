These fonts were created for my graphic experiment, which is creating hypothetical healthy snacks with eye-catching design strategy. If snacks don't cause chidren's healthy problems, the world can be a better place.
  To make these fonts, I researched about more than 80 well-known and popular snacks in the US, Europe, Japan, and Korea. These are all regions that I have lived in for at least 2 months, which allows me to experience the food culture in person.
  Yummy font series is made with a grid system inspired by Le Corbusier's modulor system. Dingbat font contains graphic components extracted from the snack packages I researched.
  Wrap slab is an ink-saving ink trap font inspired by eco paper bubble wrap made in my second semester at Bauhaus-University Weimar.
